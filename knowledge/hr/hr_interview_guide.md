# HR Interview Guide

## Tell me about yourself

A strong answer should include:
- Educational background
- Relevant skills
- Projects
- Career goals

Keep the answer between 60–90 seconds.

---

## Why should we hire you?

Mention:
- Technical skills
- Problem-solving ability
- Willingness to learn
- Teamwork
- Relevant project experience

---

## What are your strengths?

Examples:
- Quick learner
- Adaptability
- Communication
- Leadership
- Time management

Always support strengths with an example.

---

## What is your biggest weakness?

Choose a genuine weakness and explain:
- How you identified it
- What steps you are taking to improve

Avoid saying you have no weaknesses.

---

## Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

Talk about:
- Learning
- Career growth
- Increasing responsibilities
- Contributing to the organization

---

## STAR Method

Situation
Task
Action
Result

Use the STAR framework when answering behavioral interview questions.

---

## HR Evaluation Criteria

The interviewer should evaluate:
- Communication
- Confidence
- Clarity
- Relevance
- Professionalism
